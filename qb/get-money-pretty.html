Frequently asked questions

What is Money by QuickBooks? What is this app?

Money by QuickBooks is a free mobile app for new businesses or freelancers. It includes a bank account, the ability to get paid via requests and to pay other people or bills, and a debit card for any business spending.

What are the fees associated with Money by QuickBooks?

There are no monthly fees or minimum balance requirements. We do not charge to use the Pay feature or to make purchases with your debit card.


Note: ATM and limited other bank fees and payment processing fees for requests apply.

See Money by QuickBooks Deposit Account Agreement for details.

See Money by QuickBooks End User License Agreement for details.

To send and receive money, do my Contacts need the Money by QuickBooks app?

No, your Contacts do not need the app for you to pay or request money. When you send a request for payment, your contact will receive an emailed link that will allow them to pay via credit card, debit card, ACH bank transfer, Apple Pay, PayPal, or Venmo.If you want to send a payment from your Money by QuickBooks bank account, you can use the Pay feature to send your contact a check or ACH bank transfer.

Can I use Money by QuickBooks before I get my debit card?

Absolutely! You can send a request for payment to your contacts, transfer funds to and from your other bank accounts, and pay for expenses using the Pay feature.

Can I connect my QuickBooks Online account to Money by QuickBooks?

No. These are separate accounts—even though you may use the same sign in info to access both of them. As of now, you can’t connect or share data between them.


Only the funds that flow through the Money by QuickBooks app will appear in your Money by QuickBooks bank account. If you have a QuickBooks account, its data won’t appear in Money by QuickBooks app or bank account.
